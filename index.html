Mission & Vision

使命与愿景

To ensure every child possesses the fundamental ability to survive and thrive in water through continuous education.

通过持续的教育，让每个孩子都拥有基本的水上生存能力与成长机会。

Currently raising funds to support long-term community impact and water safety.

正在为长期的社区影响力与水上安全筹集资金。

Why It Matters

情绪与愿景

The Invisible Risk

In Singapore, water is everywhere, making drowning a real threat to those without swimming skills.

在新加坡，水域无处不在，缺乏基础技能意味着真实的溺水风险。

The Goal

We don't just teach strokes; we provide a "safety vest" that a child can never take off.

我们不只是教泳姿，我们是在为孩子穿上一件"永远脱不掉的救生衣"。

Impact

One child learns equals one family's peace of mind and reduces risk.

一个孩子学会游泳等于一个家庭的终身安心与风险降低。

Why Choose Us

选择我们的理由

Professional Venues

Managed by MAD/SHA Aquatic, operating in established locations such as Warren Golf & Country Club.

由 MAD/SHA Aquatic 管理，在 Warren 高尔夫俱乐部等成熟地点运营。

Systematic Excellence

We offer a structured pathway from water confidence to potential coaching or lifesaving careers.

我们提供从水中自信到未来教练或救生职业潜力的系统化培养路径。

Safety First

Our coaches are certified professionals dedicated to the highest standards of safety and youth development.

我们的教练均为持证专业人士，致力于最高标准的教学安全与青少年成长。

Execution Plan

项目运营：专业且透明

Full Transparency

Recruitment → Screening → Training → Evaluation → Reporting

招募（社区接洽）→ 筛选（资格审核）→ 训练（专业教练）→ 评估（成果展示）→ 报告（影响力回馈）

Operational Timeline

To start in June 2026 during school holiday and will run every school holiday.

12个月闭环运营，资金到位后第4个月正式开课。

Use of Funds

预算分配与筹款用途

Fundraising Purpose

Funds raised will be used to subsidize or fully support programme delivery and beneficiary support.

筹得款项将用于资助课程执行及学员支持。

Financial Commitment

Over 80% of funds go directly to coaching, pool rental, and student gear. All expenses will be accountable and recorded.

80%以上的资金直接用于教练教学、场地租赁及学员物资。

Budget Breakdown

Includes coaching fees, pool rentals, safety supervisors, and student equipment like swim caps and uniforms.

涵盖教练费、泳池租金、安全监督员及学员装备（泳帽/制服）。
